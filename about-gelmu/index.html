About Us

We love New York City and are proud to be part of what makes it shine! For decades, customers have praised and remained loyal to Gelmu’s Cleaning for their weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly needs.

Experience the difference

Customer Satisfaction

Our dedication to exceptional quality and customer satisfaction is so strong that some clients voluntarily pay more than our asking price. Their generosity allows us to maintain high standards while still offering competitive rates.

Qualified Cleaners

We never send an unqualified cleaner alone. If a cleaner doesn’t meet our standards, they assist a lead cleaner, regardless of their prior experience elsewhere.

Quality Cleaning

Gelmu's Cleaning is a family-owned housekeeping team that takes pride in providing excellent service in New York City. Our customers have trusted us as their weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly home/apartment cleaners for years.

Reasonable Prices

Our flat rates include the estimated cleaning time plus an additional 30 minutes as a contingency. Most clients don’t incur extra charges; only a few pay more for significant additional work.

Realistic Cleaning Timelines

Our estimated cleaning times are highly realistic. Thanks to thorough training and years of field experience, our cleaners consistently deliver top-notch quality within the allotted time.

Trusted by Generations

Families across New York City have trusted Gelmu’s Cleaning for decades. Our reputation is built on reliability, consistency, and a personal touch that makes every cleaning feel like a step towards a better home.
