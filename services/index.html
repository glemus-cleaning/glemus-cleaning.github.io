Standard Cleaning

Standard cleaning involves routine tasks to maintain basic cleanliness in a home or office. These tasks include:

  • Dusting surfaces
  • Vacuuming or sweeping floors
  • Mopping hard floors
  • Cleaning bathrooms
  • Wiping down kitchen surfaces
  • Emptying trash
  • Tidying up

Deep Cleaning

Deep cleaning is a more thorough cleaning process aimed at eliminating dirt, grime, and other build-up that isn't typically addressed during a standard cleaning. This is usually done less frequently and includes:

  • Detailed Dusting: Cleaning hard-to-reach areas such as vents, ceiling fans, and light fixtures. Please note, the client will need to provide a ladder for dusting high ceilings fans and light fixtures.
  • Deep Scrubbing: Removing grime from kitchen appliances (inside the oven and microwave), bathroom tiles, and grout.
  • Furniture Cleaning: Cleaning upholstery and underneath furniture.
  • Baseboards and Doors: Washing baseboards, doors, and door frames.
  • Windows: Cleaning interior windows and sturdy blinds (excluding fragile or old blinds).
  • Cabinets and Drawers: Cleaning the interiors of kitchen and bathroom cabinets and drawers.
