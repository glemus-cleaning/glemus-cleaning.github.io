Our Policies

Please review our Policies listed below.

  1. For privacy protection, customers and cleaners must communicate solely through official Gelmu's Cleaning contact channels.
  2. A confirmed job for the next day can be canceled without penalty if done before 8:00 p.m. the day before. Cancellations made after 8:00 p.m. will incur a $50 fee, which will be paid directly to the cleaner. If the job involves two cleaners, the customer will be responsible for one $50 fee, while the company will cover the additional $50 fee for the second cleaner.
  3. Whenever possible, we strive to send two or more cleaners to ensure safety, optimal quality, and faster service. Especially for first-time cleanings.
  4. We recommend specific cleaning products but are happy to accommodate your preferences. You are responsible for providing the necessary supplies; if not, we will purchase suitable products on your behalf and charge you the cost, which may include a time fee. Please let us know in advance if this is a concern so we can discuss and find a suitable solution.
  5. If you do not have a vacuum cleaner, we will bring one, but we request that you make your own available for subsequent cleanings.
  6. We will re-clean your home free of charge if you notify us within 24 hours that our work was unsatisfactory. Claims made later than 24 hours are subject to approval.
